# Management Reports (Monthly)

Vendor-neutral monthly reports based on gateway data (transactions, outcomes, fees, risk decisions, and bank routing). Reports should support filters by month, merchant, country, and bank/connector.

## 1) Monthly Transaction Volume & Value
Purpose: measure usage and growth.  
Key metrics: total transactions, total amount, split by merchant/country/bank, purchase vs cancellation.

## 2) Approval vs Decline Rate (Authorization Performance)
Purpose: understand success rate and where revenue is being lost.  
Key metrics: approval %, decline %, top decline codes, approval rate by bank/merchant.

## 3) Revenue Report – Gateway Fees
Purpose: track monthly revenue generated by the gateway.  
Key metrics: total fees per month, fees by merchant/country, average fee per transaction.

## 4) Risk & Fraud Overview
Purpose: monitor fraud controls and manual interventions.  
Key metrics: decisions (approve/review/reject), number pending review, manual cancellations, top risk reasons.

## 5) Operational Health / Connector Reliability (optional)
Purpose: identify instability affecting approvals and customer experience.  
Key metrics: timeouts/error rate, average processing time, failures by connector/bank/country.
